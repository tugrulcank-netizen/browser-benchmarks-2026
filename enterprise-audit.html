Why Organizations Are Switching to Brave

In our 2026 Enterprise Browser Survey, 67% of IT administrators cited privacy compliance as the primary driver for evaluating Brave. Other common reasons include reduced attack surface (built-in ad/tracker blocking eliminates the need for third-party extensions), lower bandwidth costs (ad blocking reduces page weight by 30-50%), and employee demand for privacy-respecting tools.
